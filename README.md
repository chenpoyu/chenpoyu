## About Me

我是 Poyu Chen，一名偏向後端、平台與系統設計的工程師。

我在意的不只是功能有沒有完成，而是它能不能被理解、被維護、被持續演進。工作上，我長期接觸內部系統、政府標案、客製化軟體、車聯網/物聯網、會員整合與零售電商這類場景；這些案子的共同點，通常是流程長、角色多、整合面廣，而且上線後還要能穩定維運。

對我來說，好的技術方案應該同時回答三件事：使用者是否真的更順、營運是否真的更清楚、工程團隊是否能長期維護。權限與身份是其中一部分，但更大的問題通常是流程、資料流、責任邊界與交付節奏。

<details>
<summary>English Version</summary>

I am Poyu Chen, an engineer focused on backend systems, platform thinking, and system design.

I care about more than shipping features. I care about whether a system can be understood, maintained, and improved over time. My work has covered internal systems, public-sector delivery projects, custom software, connected services, membership integration, and retail operations platforms.

For me, a good technical solution should balance user experience, operational clarity, delivery constraints, and long-term engineering maintainability. Identity and authorization matter, but they are part of a broader system of workflows, data flow, ownership, and operations.

</details>

## What I Work On

- **Operational Platform Architecture**: 把訂單、會員、設備事件、簽核、報表與營運流程整理成可維運的平台。
- **Internal & Government Systems**: 處理內部專案、政府標案、資安規範、文件交付與長期維護壓力。
- **System Integration**: 釐清跨系統資料流、API 邊界、同步時機、錯誤處理與責任歸屬。
- **Workflow Automation**: 把人工流程、審核節點、例外情境與營運規則整理成系統化工作流。
- **Identity & Access Management**: 在會員中心、SSO、內部系統與簽核流程中設計身份與權限邊界。
- **Product Architecture**: 從使用者、營運、工程與交付限制之間找到可落地的設計。

## Working Style

我喜歡先釐清問題的邊界，再決定技術解法。遇到混亂流程時，我通常會先把角色、狀態、資料流、權限、例外情境與驗收條件拆開，讓使用者、營運與工程能用同一張地圖討論問題。

我偏好可落地、可驗證、可持續迭代的方案。比起一次做出很大的抽象，我更重視每一步是否能降低未來維護成本，是否能支撐上線、驗收、維運與後續擴充。

<details>
<summary>English Version</summary>

I like to clarify boundaries before choosing a technical solution. When a process is messy, I tend to separate roles, states, data flow, permissions, edge cases, and acceptance criteria first, so users, operations, and engineers can reason about the same model.

I prefer practical, verifiable, and iterative solutions. Instead of introducing large abstractions too early, I care about whether each step reduces long-term maintenance cost and supports delivery, acceptance, operations, and future expansion.

</details>
