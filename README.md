## What I Build

### 🔐 Identity & Access Management

設計身份、驗證與授權流程，讓產品和團隊成長後，權限邊界仍然清楚。<br/>
Designing authentication and authorization systems that stay maintainable as products and teams grow.

常用技術：`Java` · `.NET Core` · `PostgreSQL` · `IAM` · `SSO` · `Keycloak`

### ⚙️ Internal Platforms

打造內部營運平台與工具，把跨部門流程、資料狀態與日常作業變得更容易追蹤和改善。<br/>
Building operational tools that make complex workflows easier to run, observe, and improve.

常用技術：`ReactJS` · `.NET Core` · `PostgreSQL` · `Redis`

### 🔄 Workflow Automation

把重複、容易漏接的營運流程，整理成可靠、可追蹤、能持續迭代的系統。<br/>
Turning repetitive operational processes into reliable, traceable systems.

常用技術：`Docker` · `GitHub Actions` · `API Design` · `System Integration`
